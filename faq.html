For implant cases we recommend the 4D PRO or 3D Plus Multilayer. The 4D PRO offers the widest strength gradient (700–1200 MPa) and is specifically designed for full-arch implant work. The 3D Plus delivers maximum 1200 MPa throughout, ideal for high-stress implant crowns.
Yes — all multilayer discs are available in the full Vita Classical shade range (A1–A4, B1–B4, C1–C4, D2–D4, BL1–BL4). Shade does not affect the price. You pay the same price for any shade of a given product and size. ST/HT White discs are available in white only.
Payment terms are discussed individually per order. We work with bank transfer and other options. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to discuss what works best for you.
ST (Standard Translucency) discs have higher strength and are suited for posterior restorations and bridges where durability is the priority. HT (High Translucency) discs have greater light transmission, making them better suited for anterior restorations where aesthetics matter most. Both are available in the White monolithic version.
Most Yucera discs sinter at 1530°C (ST Multilayer, SHT Multilayer, 3D Plus, ST/HT White). The 4D PRO, 3D Nature, and Pro Master sinter at 1500°C. Always check the product spec before sintering.
