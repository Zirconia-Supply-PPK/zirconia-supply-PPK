Who We Are

Connecting Dental Labs with Premium Materials

Zirconia Supply PPK was started with one simple goal: to make it easier for dental laboratories across Europe to access high-quality zirconia discs at fair prices, without the complexity of dealing directly with manufacturers on the other side of the world.

Based in Almaty, Kazakhstan, we source Yucera zirconia discs — one of the most trusted names in dental milling materials — and deliver them to labs across Europe. Yucera products carry ISO 13485, CE 1639, and FDA certification, meaning you get consistent, clinically reliable material every time.

We keep things personal and straightforward. No complex procurement processes — just a direct message on WhatsApp or an email, and we handle the rest. Payment terms are discussed individually, and we're always happy to answer technical questions about the materials.

Whether you run a small boutique lab or a high-volume operation, we can supply what you need — from economical ST/HT White monolithics to premium 4D PRO multilayers for complex implant cases.
